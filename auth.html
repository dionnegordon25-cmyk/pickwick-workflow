DG Lettings — Outlook Authorisation

One-time setup to connect your Outlook account to the automated email system. Click the button below to log in with your Microsoft account. You only need to do this once.

Step 1 — AuthoriseClick the button below and log in with your Pickwick Estates Microsoft account
Step 2 — Copy the refresh tokenAfter login, your refresh token will appear below. Copy it.
Step 3 — Save to NetlifyGo to Netlify → Site settings → Environment variables → add OUTLOOK_REFRESH_TOKEN with the copied value.

Paste this as OUTLOOK_REFRESH_TOKEN in Netlify environment variables. The automation will use this to send emails on your behalf.
